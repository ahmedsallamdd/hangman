How to play:

The game has two modes: "Single player", "Multi player"

1. Single player mode:
In this mode, you can choose from 4 categories: phrases, movies, songs and challenges.

Each of these categories has pre-defined lists of items related to that category.
An item is selected randomly and creates spaces on top for the gusssed letters. Everytime you guess a letter right, its corresponding space is filled by that letter.
you will get only 8 false trials or else you lose, so choose wisely.

2. Multi player mode:
This mode requires two players. The first one enters the name of a movie of their choice and clicks submit -while the other player is not looking ofcourse-, then the other player tries to guess the name of that movie.
The maximum number of false gusses is also 8, just as the prevoious mode.
